Endnadered Sea Turtle

New info

Human activities have tipped the scales against the survival of sea turtles. Nearly all
species of sea turtle are classified as Endangered. Slaughtered for their eggs, meat, skin
and shells, sea turtles suffer from poaching and over-exploitation. They also face habitat
destruction and accidental capture in fishing gear. Climate change has an impact on
turtle nesting sites. It alters sand temperatures, which then affects the sex of hatchlings.
